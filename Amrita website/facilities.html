Facilities at Amrita

Labs

Our state-of-the-art labs are equipped with the latest technology and tools, providing students with hands-on experience and practical learning opportunities. Whether you're exploring intricate experiments in the field of chemistry or developing cutting-edge software applications in computer science, our labs offer an environment where creativity and innovation thrive. Our dedicated lab instructors are always available to guide you through your experiments, ensuring you gain a deep understanding of theoretical concepts through real-world applications.

Libraries

Step into our libraries and embark on a journey of knowledge and discovery. Our libraries are more than just physical spaces; they are a treasure trove of information, offering an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources to support research and learning. Whether you're pursuing in-depth research in your chosen field or looking for leisure reading, you'll find a wide range of resources that cater to your interests. Our librarians are always ready to assist you in finding the right material and making the most of the wealth of knowledge at your fingertips.

Sports Facilities

At our institution, we believe in fostering a holistic approach to education, which includes promoting physical well-being through our top-notch sports facilities. Stay active and healthy with access to our well-equipped gym, outdoor sports grounds, and indoor courts. Whether you're a dedicated athlete looking to refine your skills or simply want to engage in recreational activities, our sports facilities cater to all levels of interest and expertise. Physical activity not only promotes a healthy lifestyle but also instills values like teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, all of which contribute to your personal and academic growth.
